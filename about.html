About Us

S&R Membership Shopping is a premium warehouse club in the Philippines. Our mission is to be an essential part of our members' lives by providing high-quality imported products and services at the best prices. With 22 warehouse branches and 51 Quick Service Restaurants (QSRs) nationwide, we offer world-class shopping experiences.

At S&R, you'll find a wide range of imported products from around the world. From food, wines, and liquor to furniture, appliances, health and beauty products, and household items, we have it all. We are constantly expanding, with plans to open new warehouses and more QSRs to provide even better deals and value for our members.

Inside our stores, you'll also discover additional amenities. Select clubs offer Food Service, where you can enjoy our famous S&R New York Style Pizza, Southern Style Fried Chicken, All Beef Hotdog, Cheeseburger, French Fries, and more. The Deli Corner features premium coffee, delicious cakes and pastries, and imported ice cream. In select clubs, our Tire Center offers free automotive tire services, including mounting and installation, valves, weights, nitrogen tire inflation, and balancing and wheel weights.
